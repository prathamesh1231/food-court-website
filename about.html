Our Story

Prathamesh Food-court was born from a simple love — the love of good food shared with good people.

In 2020, that dream became reality. Starting with just three items on the menu — a loaded cheese pizza, a crispy veg burger, and a classic sandwich — the little stall near the HP Petrol Pump quickly became the talk of the town.

Today, Prathamesh Food-court serves hundreds of happy customers every week. The menu has grown, the space has expanded, but the soul remains the same: fresh ingredients, honest prices, and food made with love.
